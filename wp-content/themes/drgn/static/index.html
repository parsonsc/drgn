About DRGN

DRGN is the world’s first deluxe Fusion Drink, inspired by hedonistic playgrounds from the Far East to the ‘wild’ West. DRGN can be consumed on its own or as a revolutionary new mixer - the ultimate functional drink for a smarter way to party.

Infused with Turmeric, Ginseng and Ginger Root, and enhanced with Vitamins and Electrolytes, DRGN helps to metabolize toxins, whilst providing a natural energy boost and rehydration when you need it most. Asia’s best kept secret is now Hedonism’s Holy Grail.
